## 9. Valuation Models

### 9.1 Early-Stage Technology Valuation

**Option-Based Valuation:**
- Technology as real option
- Value = max(Market Value - Development Cost, 0)
- Accounts for uncertainty and flexibility

**Risk-Adjusted NPV:**
- Probability-weighted scenarios
- High discount rates (30-50% for early stage)
- Monte Carlo simulation

**Comparable Transactions:**
- Similar-stage technologies
- Acquisition multiples
- Licensing deals

### 9.2 Conservative Valuation Estimate

**Assuming ATOMIC_EX_LIGHT is feasible:**

**Scenario Analysis:**

1. **Success (10% probability):**
   - Market: $100B+ (nuclear waste management)
   - Company capture: 5-10% market share
   - Value: $5B-$10B
   
2. **Partial Success (20% probability):**
   - Niche applications
   - Limited adoption
   - Value: $500M-$1B

3. **Failure (70% probability):**
   - Technology doesn't work
   - Value: $0

**Expected Value:** 0.1 × $7.5B + 0.2 × $750M + 0.7 × $0 = $900M

**Risk-Adjusted (50% discount rate):** ~$50M-$100M current value
